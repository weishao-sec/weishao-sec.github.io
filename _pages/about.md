I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of California, Davis, advised by Prof. Houman Homayoun and Prof. Setareh Rafatirad.

My research focuses on systems security, particularly isolation, authentication, and resource management for emerging and heterogeneous computing platforms, including wearables, cloud systems, and GPUs.
